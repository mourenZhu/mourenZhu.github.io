不规则变化的比较级和最高级

好，坏，多，少，老，远
|原级|比较级|最高级|
|—-|—-|—-|
|good / well | better | best |
|bad / ill | worse | worst |
|many / much | more | most |
|littler / a little | less | least |
|old | older | oldest |
| | elder | eldest(专指人) |
|far | farther | farthest(专指距离) |
| | further | furthest(抽象意义上的远) |
